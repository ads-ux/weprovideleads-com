Common Questions

Questions about our lead generation service

Straight answers about how WeProvideLeads works for Guelph home service contractors.

How do you generate the leads?

We own 28 dedicated home service websites targeting Guelph and Wellington County. Each site ranks in Google Search for its trade category. When a homeowner fills out a contact form, that inquiry is forwarded to you in real time.

Are the leads exclusive to me?

Yes. We work with one contractor per trade category per area. Your leads are not resold or shared with competitors. When you’re our roofing partner, you’re the only roofer who receives leads from our Guelph roofing site.

What information comes with each lead?

Name, phone number, email, service address, trade category, and any notes the homeowner left about their project — enough to call and book a quote immediately.

How are inquiries delivered?

Three ways: phone calls and voicemails route directly to your business line; contact form submissions and emails forward to your inbox within minutes. We configure the routing when you sign on — you just answer.

What areas do the leads come from?

Guelph and surrounding Wellington County communities — Fergus, Elora, Rockwood, Erin, Puslinch, and Centre Wellington. All within typical service radius for most Guelph-based tradespeople.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

No long-term contracts. Monthly billing — cancel any time. We’d rather earn your continued business by delivering quality inquiries than lock you into a term.

How does pricing work?

We charge a flat monthly fee per trade category. Pricing is custom per trade based on average job value and typical inquiry volume. Fill out the quote form or call us and we’ll send you a custom quote within one business day.

How do I get started?

Fill out the quote form below or call (519) 942-5698. We’ll confirm whether the category is still open, send custom pricing, and get you set up — usually within a day.

Can I get leads for more than one trade?

Not through the same territory — but if you operate two separate businesses or divisions in different trades (roofing and gutters, for example), we can set up separate arrangements. Each trade slot is held by one entity and billed independently.

What’s the difference between your service and HomeStars or Thumbtack?

HomeStars and Thumbtack sell the same lead to multiple contractors at once. Our model works differently: we own trade-specific ranking websites and route every inquiry to one partner. You’re not competing against four other roofers for the same homeowner — you’re the only one who hears from them.

How long does it take to start receiving leads?

Most partners receive their first inquiry within the first week of setup. Our sites are already ranking in Google before you sign on — we don’t need to build or rank anything from scratch. We configure the call routing and email forwarding to your contact info and you’re live.

What happens if a trade category is already taken in my area?

We’ll let you know which categories are open when you reach out. If your trade is filled, you can join a waitlist and we’ll contact you when the slot opens. We don’t stack partners or sell to multiple contractors in the same trade — so availability is real.
