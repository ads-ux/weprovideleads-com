Common Questions

What contractors usually ask

Straight answers about how this works.

How do you generate the leads?

We own 10 dedicated home service websites targeting Guelph and Wellington County. Each site ranks in Google Search for its trade category. When a homeowner fills out a contact form, that inquiry is forwarded to you in real time.

Are the leads exclusive to me?

Yes. We work with one contractor per trade category per area. Your leads are not resold or shared with competitors. When you're our roofing partner, you're the only roofer who receives leads from our roofing site.

What information comes with each lead?

Name, phone number, email, address, service type, and any notes the homeowner left about their project. Enough to call and book a quote immediately.

How are leads delivered?

By email in real time as soon as the homeowner submits the form. We can also set up call forwarding so inbound calls route directly to your business line.

What areas do the leads come from?

Guelph and surrounding Wellington County communities — Fergus, Elora, Rockwood, Erin, Puslinch, and Centre Wellington. All within typical service radius for most Guelph-based tradespeople.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

No long-term contracts and no minimums. Pay per lead, pause or stop any time. We'd rather earn your continued business by delivering quality leads than lock you into a term.

How much do leads cost?

Pricing is custom per trade based on average job value and typical lead volume for that category. Fill out the quote form or call us and we'll send you a custom quote within one business day.

How do I get started?

Fill out the quote form below or call (226) 887-5522. We'll confirm whether the category is still open, send custom pricing, and get you set up — usually within a day.
