How WeProvideLeads Works

We don’t buy ads and resell clicks, and we don’t broker shared leads. We own the local websites that rank in Google for your trade in Guelph — and we send the homeowners who find them to one contractor.

Most lead-generation companies fall into one of two camps. Ad agencies run paid campaigns and bill you for clicks, whether or not those clicks ever become customers. Lead brokers collect homeowner information and sell the same lead to four or five contractors at once, so you race competitors to the phone and win maybe one job in five. WeProvideLeads is built on a different model entirely.

We build and own the websites

We have built 28 dedicated home service websites targeting Guelph and Wellington County — one per trade. These are real websites with unique content, blog posts, local schema markup, Google Business Profiles, and Google Search Console verification. They rank organically because we invested in them, not because we’re renting ad space. Several already sit at #1 or on Page 1 of Google for their core search terms.

Homeowners find us through Google

When a Guelph homeowner searches for a service — “basement waterproofing Guelph,” “mold removal Guelph,” “Guelph fencing” — our dedicated site for that trade ranks and they land on it. They read about the service, then call the number or fill out the contact form. That’s a fundamentally higher-intent action than clicking an ad: they searched, evaluated, and chose to reach out.

The inquiry goes to you — exclusively

Phone calls and voicemails route directly to your business line. Contact form submissions and emails forward to your inbox within minutes, complete with the homeowner’s name, phone, address, trade category, and project notes. Because we work with only one contractor per trade per area, that lead is yours alone. We never resell it or share it with a competitor.

You pay a percentage of the completed job

We charge a percentage of the finished job value per trade category — not a flat monthly fee, not a per-lead charge. When a lead we send you closes, you pay that percentage of the final invoice; if it doesn’t close, you owe nothing. Rates vary by trade based on typical job value in Guelph. No setup fees, no long-term contracts, cancel any time.
